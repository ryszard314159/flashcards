🎴 Flashcard Pro Guide

Master your study session with these shortcuts.

👆 Interaction

  • Flip: Quick tap/click the card center.
  • Copy: Long-press (Mobile) or Drag (Desktop) the text.
  • Navigate: Use the colored side zones (Next/Prev).

📈 Frequency (±)

Use the plus and minus buttons to tell the app which cards are hard. Higher frequency means the card will appear more often in your shuffle.

📂 Decks & Folders

  • Import: Load local .deck files.
  • Remote: Browse the GitHub repository for new content.
  • Navigation: Use 📁 to enter and ⬅️ to go back.

Tip: Enable "Auto-Play" in settings to hear audio immediately upon flipping!
